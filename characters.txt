==========
CHARACTERS
==========

Jay Gatsby - a young, mysterious millionaire. Loves Daisy Buchanan.

Nick Carraway - Our narrator and guide.

Daisy Buchanan - a beautiful woman from Louisville.

Thomas "Tom" Buchanan- a millionaire who lives in East Egg, and Daisy's husband.

Myrtle Wilson -
Tom's lover, whose lifeless husband George owns a run-down garage in the valley of ashes.

George Wilson - Myrtle's husband, the lifeless, exhausted owner of a run-down auto shop at the edge of the valley. George loves and idealizes Myrtle, and is devastated by her affair with Tom.
